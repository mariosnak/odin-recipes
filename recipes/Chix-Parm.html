Chicken Parm Recipe

Description

Chicken parmesan, or chicken parm, is a classic Italian-American dish consisting of breaded and fried chicken cutlets topped with tomato sauce and mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. It's a popular comfort food, often served with pasta. The dish evolved from eggplant parmigiana, another Italian classic, and became popular in the United States during the early 20th century.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2-3 eggs
  • 1-1.5 cups breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2-3 cups marinara sauce
  • 1-2 cups shredded mozzarella
  • Handful of fresh basil

Instructions

For the Chicken:

  1. Boneless, skinless chicken breasts: 2-3 pounds, sliced in half horizontally (or pounded to 1/4 inch thickness).
  2. All-purpose flour: 1/2 cup.
  3. Eggs: 2-3 large, beaten.
  4. Italian breadcrumbs or Panko breadcrumbs: 1-1.5 cups total, combined with Parmesan cheese.
  5. Grated Parmesan cheese: 1/2 cup, for the breading mixture.
  6. Salt and freshly ground black pepper: To taste.

For the Sauce and Toppings:

  • Olive oil: 1/4 cup for frying the chicken.
  • Marinara sauce: 2-3 cups, homemade or store-bought.
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese: 1-2 cups.
  • Fresh basil leaves: For garnish, optional.

Optional additions:

  • Italian seasoning or other herbs can be added to the breadcrumb mixture.
  • Provolone cheese can be used instead of or in addition to mozzarella.
